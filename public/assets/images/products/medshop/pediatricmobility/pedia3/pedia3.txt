AkcesMed Nova Evo Stroller
NOVA® is a cosy special needs stroller for people with locomotor disabilities. It is especially recommended for spastic users as well as those requiring additional stabilization.
The stroller is easy to drive on both flat and uneven terrain. Thanks to its simple and lightweight design, the NOVA® stroller can be easily folded for transportation.

SPECIFICATION

 Seat width[cm]: 31-41
 Seat depth[cm]: 32-44
 Maximum user weight[kg]: 40-90
