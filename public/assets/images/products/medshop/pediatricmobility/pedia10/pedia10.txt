AkcesMed Smart Standing Frame
Standing frame SMART™ combines the advantages of low weight, ease of use, robust design and stability. SMART™ gives the user a sense of safety and great freedom of movement within the upper limbs. The standing frame uses an innovative system UPRIGHT PRO™ – consisting of chest and pelvic braces with a very wide range of adjustment that enables to adopt the proper position.

 Max. user weight[kg]: 35-95cm
