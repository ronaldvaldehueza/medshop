AkcesMed Ulises Evo Stroller
ULISES EVO™ is a perfect stroller designed for daily rehabilitation of children and youth with locomotor dysfunctions. The stroller has adjustable seat depth and backrest tilt angle which greatly facilitates its functionality. With simple and light construction it can be easily folded for transporting purposes. Furthermore, relatively small size allows you to fit ULISES EVO™ into an undersized car trunk.

SPECIFICATIONS

 Seat width[cm]: 31-43
 Seat depth[cm]: 22-45
 Maximum user weight[kg]: 55-75
