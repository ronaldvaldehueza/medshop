DETAILS

The belt has four sets of handles, placed to give the user a choice of lifting positions and to accommodate the differing sizes of patient

It is made from soft, padded materials with strong handles and patented eco PER anti-slip mesh on the back area, providing stronger, safer and easier patient handling

It enables caregiver to maintain a safe posture reducing work-related injury
